Strategy &

Planning

With a rock-solid strategy, we create trustworthy identities, compelling experiences, and engaging content to help brands make a delightful impact on their users, stakeholders, and their bottom line.

At Enigma, strategic planning based on insight is the starting point of everything we do. We consider a sound Digital Strategy to be the backbone of every brand’s online presence. Using a blend of strategic thinking, broad marketing experience, insights, proven best practices, and sound judgment, we create effective marketing strategies that transform insights into actionable plans to deliver measurable results.

It It Our digital strategy development process is thorough, enlightening and stimulating, helping you find insights and clarity along the way. Our analysts, planners and researchers’ approach is to begin by consulting with you. After all, no one can know more about your product & brand better than you! We gain as much insight and understanding as possible during the early stages, taking in data from wherever necessary. Thereafter, we determine the message you are trying to send to your audience, your product, the market and the competition. This forms the basis of our strategic thinking, idea generation, experience planning, communication and campaign plans.
